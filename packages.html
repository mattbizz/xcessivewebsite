Our Music Video Process

1. First, we get on a call and meet with you to discuss ideas

2. Your A/V Script will written by our Director(s)

3. We conduct a secondary meeting confirming you agree with our A/V Script

4. Props are purchased(if necessary)

5. Your music video is filmed at your desired location

6. We take the footage and edit it to our expertise

7. We conduct a final meeting confirming the music video with you(and making changes if necessary)

8. Your music video is released on our YouTube page!
